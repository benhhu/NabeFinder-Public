A web applications to find best neighborhoods for people with disabilities, built with Bootstrap 3, Leaflet, Flask, and typeahead.js. Open source and available on GitHub.

Features
  • Gives personalized predictions on income and probability of employment and health.
  • Includes 15 neighborhood indicators.
  • Weights determined by users' preferences.
